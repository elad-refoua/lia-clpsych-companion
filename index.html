Talking with Lia

A persona simulation for practicing reflective parenting in wartime

Lia is a seven-year-old who has just come home from school. There was an air-raid siren during recess; her teacher said the school costume party is cancelled. Below are two recorded sessions with the same Lia, in which the parent's responses differ in skill. The simulation's character does not yield to the parent — she responds to what is actually offered.
Session 1

A parent who reflects

2:04 · silent video, ambient score

The parent stays close to what Lia brings — validating, sitting with the complexity, naming feelings without rushing to fix. Lia opens slowly.

Session 2

A parent who is overwhelmed

2:04 · silent video, ambient score

The same Lia, with a tired parent who slips into dismissal and false reassurance. The conversation closes — and then, late, repairs.
